# Stay Safe Online: Prevention Checklist for College Students in India

- Verify caller identity before sharing details—real officials never demand instant UPI payments or "digital arrests."
- Enable UPI PIN alerts and two-factor authentication (2FA) on banking apps like Google Pay/PhonePe.
- **For UPI safety: Never share UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details via call/WhatsApp—banks don't ask this.**
- **For UPI safety: Set daily UPI transaction limits (e.g., Rs 5,000) via app settings to cap losses.**
- Use official apps only; avoid links from unknown SMS/calls claiming account issues.
- Install reputable antivirus (e.g., Quick Heal) and keep phone OS updated to block phishing malware.
- Educate friends/roommates on scams during group chats—peer awareness prevents "friend-shared account" frauds.
- Log suspicious transactions immediately in bank app and freeze UPI if needed.
- Report incidents fast: Visit cybercrime.gov.in or call Helpline 1930 for free guidance and FIR filing.
