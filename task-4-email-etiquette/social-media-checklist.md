## Social Media Do's and Don'ts

### Do's
- Use your real name and a simple, professional username on platforms you might share with teachers or recruiters.
- Think before posting, ask yourself if you had be comfortable with your post being seen by faculty or future employers.
- Check and regularly update privacy settings so you control who sees your content.
- Share achievements like projects, certificates and hackathon experiences to build a positive digital footprint
- Be respectful in comments and DMs, avoid trolling, personal attacks or insensitive jokes.

### Dont'ts
- Don't post personal details like home address, phone number or class schedule publicly.
- Don't upload photos or reels that show cheating, substance abuse or anything that could damage your reputation.
- Don't share screenshots of private chats without permission.
- Don't spread unverified news or rumours always check credible sources first
- Don't argue aggressively online, if a discussion gets heated, take a break or move it to a calmer, private channel.
